What is Photosynthesis?

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants, algae, and some bacteria convert light energy into chemical energy stored in glucose. It occurs in the chloroplasts of plant cells, where sunlight is absorbed by chlorophyll and other pigments. The process can be summarized in the following steps:

Steps of Photosynthesis:

  • Step 1: Light absorption by chlorophyll.
  • Step 2: Conversion of light energy into chemical energy.
  • Step 3: Formation of glucose (C₆H₁₂O₆).
  • Step 4: Oxygen is released as a byproduct.
